Spoiler: New MLW World Middleweight Champion Crowned At MLW Super Series

A new World Middleweight Champion was crowned at MLW’s Super Series taping.

Major League Wrestling made its Atlanta debut on September 18, and though the Super Series shows won’t air until the new season of MLW Fusion begins, the company has announced one of the major outcomes of the taping. At the show on Sunday night, DRAGONGATE star Shun Skywalker defeated Myron Reed to win the MLW World Middleweight Championship, ending Reed’s third reign with the gold. He previously won the title at the Blood and Thunder taping in January.

From MLW:

Breaking news: there is a new World Middleweight Champion!

Last night in Atlanta history was made as Shun Skywalker pinned Myron Reed to end his 9-month reign.

Cameras were rolling as the title changed hands, the first MLW title to do so in Georgia. The title bout will air this fall when the new season of FUSION kicks off.

League officials are set for a zoom call with DRAGONGATE CEO Mr. Kido to map out upcoming title bouts for the new champion.
