Bachelorette Katie Thurston wows in the dreamiest metallic gown

The reality star stunned in the look on the show Monday night

    As fans tune in each week to see which guy will win Katie Thurston’s heart, we’re keeping an eye on her stellar style. The former Bachelor star stunned in a metallic green one-sleeve Ong-Oaj Pairam draped lamé gown on the show Monday night and when we spotted her in the look, we couldn’t stop swooning. 

    The dress is dropped by hand to flatter the figure and has ruffles at the waist to accentuate curves. It was stunning, so much so, that even with a hefty price tag of $4,420, it’s already sold out.
