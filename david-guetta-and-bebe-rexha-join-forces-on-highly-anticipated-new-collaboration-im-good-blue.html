David Guetta and Bebe Rexha join forces on highly anticipated new collaboration Im Good (Blue)

After having started working together on this song five years ago, global superstars David Guetta and Bebe Rexha, have joined forces to bring fans worldwide a brand new anticipated new single ‘I’m Good (Blue)’.

Already a TikTok smash hit, the sound alone has amassed over 130,000 total creations and 500 million views, as well as being a hit with YouTube recreations across the world. Bebe and David’s online buzz is finally now ready for an official release. This is the type of song that showcases Bebe’s soulful vocals with euphoric piano chords and energy.

This song follows a prolific run of singles from David, including a rework of ‘Satisfaction’ with Benny Benassi and the anthem ‘Family Affair (Dance For Me)’, which has achieved over 10 million streams since its release.

Releasing cutting-edge track after cutting-edge track, there is a reason why David Guetta is widely considered to be the most complete artist in dance music today.
