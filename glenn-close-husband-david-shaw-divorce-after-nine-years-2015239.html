Glenn Close, Husband David Shaw Divorce After Nine Years

Another one bites the dust. Glenn Close and her husband, David Shaw, have quietly divorced, her rep confirmed to Page Six. The Fatal Attraction actress, 68, and Shaw were married for nine years before calling it quits.

According to Page Six, Close and Shaw separated awhile ago.

“Glenn and David have been telling friends over the summer they are divorcing, and he’ll remain living in Maine while Glenn moves back to New York full-time,” a source told the site, adding that the six-time Oscar winner plans to focus on her career. “They’ve been separated for some time, and things are amicable between them.”
