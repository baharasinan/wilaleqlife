Lea Michele Shows Off Toned Body in Bikini at Coachella: Pics

???

A post shared by Lea Michele (@leamichele) on Apr 16, 2016 at 12:04pm PDT

Damn, girl! Lea Michele showed off her toned body and killer long legs in a bikini at Coachella on Saturday, April 16 — and she looked amazing.

The Glee alum, 29, rocked the Solid & Striped Poppy Wrap Bikini Top and Poppy Tie Bottom while lounging in the sun on a hammock during a trip to Indio, California, for the star-studded music festival.

Michele captioned the sexy shot with three simple — but fitting — emojis: a palm tree, a cactus and a pink flower.

“Made it to Coachella,” she posted on Instagram earlier in the day. “SO excited to be here!!!”

The actress later shared a series of photos from the event, including one shot of her braided hair and stylish monochromatic outfit.

Earlier in the week, Michele debuted a tattoo of the number 5 that she got in honor of her late boyfriend and former costar, Cory Monteith, on the side of her rib cage. Five was the number on the back of Monteith’s character Finn Hudson’s football jersey on Glee.

“And one more.. for my Quarterback.. #5,” she captioned the sweet image.
