LPBW: Will Molly Roloff Be Announcing A Pregnancy Soon?

Little People, Big World has been on the air for a whopping 21 seasons, originally airing in 2006. That means we’ve seen years and years of the Roloff family’s growth and changes, including their family growing larger as they marry and have children.

But now, their children have children!

Even though many of the Roloffs have begun to expand their family, not all of them have become parents, including Molly Roloff, who was married in 2017. But is she the next to have a child?
