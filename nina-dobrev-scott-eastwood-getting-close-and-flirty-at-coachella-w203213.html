Nina Dobrev, Scott Eastwood 'Getting Close and Flirty' at Coachella

Cozying up with a vampire! Scott Eastwood and Nina Dobrev had eyes only for each other as they cozied up together at Coachella’s Neon Carnival on Saturday, April 16.

Dobrev, 27, who split from Austin Stowell earlier this year, has been friends with Eastwood for some time, posing for a photo with the 30-year-old actor that he shared via Instagram after the Teen Choice Awards eight months ago.

But judging by the way they were interacting at the music festival’s star-studded late-night afterparty over the weekend, the buddies have taken their relationship to the next level and were spotted having a lot of flirty fun as they rode rides together at the carnival.

“Scott and Nina were getting very close,” one source told Us Weekly of the cute duo. “Very flirty.”

“[They] were super cozy doing the bumper cars,” added another.

In fact, even Eastwood admitted he was “Having a fun time at Coachella with this vampire” in a caption to a photograph he shared via Facebook of the two of them, arms wrapped around each other, at the event, punctuating his comment with a winking face emoji!

Turns out the Neon Carnival was quite the place to be to spot some exciting celebrity pairings this weekend. The Thermal, California, bash was also the venue for Rihanna and Leonardo DiCaprio‘s reunion.

As Us reported previously the pair were snapped standing close together, the former Titanic star whispering in Rihanna’s ear as she smiled.
