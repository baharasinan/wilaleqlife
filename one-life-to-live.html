One Life To Live

  • One Life To Live

The soap opera, One Life to Live made its debut on July 15, 1968, on ABC where it stayed until January 13, 2012, when the show was canceled before moving to Hulu for one season from April 29, 2013 to August 19, 2013. The show's storyline focused on the Buchanan, Lord and Manning families and the show was set in the fictional city of Llanview, Pennsylvania, which is a suburb of Philadelphia.
