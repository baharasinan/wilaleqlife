Rose Byrne Is Dating Bobby Cannavale!

There's a new man in Rose Byrne's life: Bobby Cannavale, star of HBO's critically-acclaimed drama Boardwalk Empire!

"They've been dating for a few months," a source tells Us Weekly. "I think Rose is a good match for him."

The actors were spotted packing on the PDA at the Killing Them Softly afterparty at No. 8 in New York City Nov. 26. "They were sitting in a booth and he had his arm around her and they would kiss from time to time," a fellow attendee reveals. "They definitely tried to keep a low profile and left at separate times."
