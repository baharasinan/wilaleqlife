Sarah Reeves XMAS Album! - CelebMix

 “It’s TIMEEE,” as Mariah Carey likes to announce every November, the most amazing time of the year has come again. The annual battle for the charting number one spot has begun. 

The 2021 genre entry Sarah Reeves has surprised everyone with her tireless talent for producing seasonal cheers. Her debut Christmas album ‘More The Merrier’ had it all: the classics, warmth, and original songwriting.

With a new deluxe release, Sarah Reeves offers new versions with a jazzy spruce-up  of ‘All I Want For Christmas’ and ‘Last Christmas.’ What makes Sarah Reeves stand out is her original productions such as ‘Snow Globe,’ ‘The Greatest Gift,’ ‘Christmas Feels Different This Year’ and the title track.

Following up on the release of her pop record ‘Best Days,’ Sarah Reeves demonstrates significant growth as it is the first album she wrote and produced entirely herself. ‘Best Days’ is a pure pop perfection. 

Extending the holiday cheer, ‘More The Merrier’  is more than a collection of Christmas covers. It is a cosy sonic blanket, with Reeves’ warm, melancholic vocals accompanying you throughout the holidays. 

Stream ‘More The Merrier (Deluxe Version)’ is out now.
