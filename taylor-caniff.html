Taylor Caniff Is a 1996s Kid!!! Early Life And Family

Taylor Caniff, aka Taylor Michael Caniff, was born on 3 February 1996 in Princeton, Indiana, the United States, to father Kevin Caniff and his wife. 

He grew up in his hometown with his parents and three other siblings: brothers Tristen & Jordan and his sister Kellie Caniff Bruce. 

As of 2021, Taylor Caniff is 25 years old. He holds an American nationality and belongs to a white ethnic background. 

He has a height of 5 feet 8 inches (172 cm) and weighs around 70 kg (154 pounds). 

Career Highlights And Net Worth 

Taylor Caniff started his professional career with the comedy platform Vines. 

On 2 September 2012, Caniff started his self-titled YouTube channel. Initially, he posted six seconds of prank clips and comedy sketches. 

From his YouTube channel, he earns an estimated monthly income of $2 to $29 and yearly earnings of $22 to $354. 

Later, he joined a Meet and Greet Convention touring group, Magcon touring group with members like Cameron Dalla, Shawn Mendes, Nas Grier, Aaron Carpenter, Jack & Jack, and others. He traveled with the group until 2014. 

After the tour ended, the group disbanded. Taylor then joined DIgiTour. While staying with the DigiTour, he got his first solo tour in 2015. A year later, in 2016, Caniff, Aaron Carpenter, Dylan Dauzat, Cameron Dallas, and others rebooted the Magcon. 

In the same year, YouTube sensation landed a role in the Netflix original reality series Chasing Cameron. The series centered around social media influencer Cameron Dallas and premiered on 27 December 2016. He also appeared in an American TV late-night talk show, Chelsea.  

Besides that, Taylor Caniff is also a musician. He formed a hip hop duo, 2Virgins, with Dillon Rupp in 2014. The duo released their first single, Buckwild, on 5 April 2014. In 2015, their second album named Like Whoo, was released, and a year later, his third song, Cash On Me, was released.
