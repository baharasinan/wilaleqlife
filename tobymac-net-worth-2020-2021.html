Career

The musical journey of Tobymac kicked off in 1987 with the inception of the group DC Talk. He co-founded the group together with Michael Tait, and they later recruited former classmate Kevin Max to the group in 1988.

Their debut album DC Talk was released in 1989, and after that, they set out on tour. Their first gold album titled Nu Thang was released in 1990. In 1995 they released an album titled Jesus Freak, and it as well went multi-platinum. Almost all of the albums the duo released were very successful. In 2001 DC Talk disbanded, and all the members embarked on a solo career.

The solo career of Tobymac kicked off with the release of his first album called Momentum, which he released in 2001. The album debuted at number one on the Billboard Heatseekers chart. The single Extreme Days from the album were used in the movie titled Hangman’s Curse. Another single Get This Party Started was featured in the Roswell TV show.

In October 2004, he released his next album, which was titled Welcome to Diverse City, and it peaked on the Billboard chart at number 54. The album was also certified gold, and it sold more than 21,000 copies in the first week. The following albums were also very successful, and they included Portable Sounds in 2007, Alive and Transported in 2008, Tonight in 2010, Eye on It, This was not a Test in 2015, and The Elements in 2018.

As an author, he has co-authored two books with Michael Tait and Kevin Max. The books are Jesus Freaks: Revolutionaries: Stories of Revolutionaries Who Changed Their World: Fearing GOD, Not Man, and Jesus Freaks: DC Talk and The Voice of the Martyrs- Stories of Those Who Stood for Jesus, the Ultimate Jesus Freaks. His other books are Under God, Living Under God: Discovering Your Part in God’s Plan, and City on Our Knees.

Tobymac is one of the founders of a camp located in Nashville, Tennessee, aimed at helping young musicians who are interested in pop and rock music. The camp is called Camp Electric. He is also the president of a record label known as Gotee Records.

Awards & Achievements

Tobymac has been entertaining us with his Christian music for several years, and he has won several prestigious awards.
