Who Was Cara Williams Ex-Husband John Drew Barrymore?

John Drew Barrymore was the ex-husband of American actress, Cara Williams. They were married from 1952 to 1959.

Who was John Drew Barrymore?

John Drew Barrymore was an American film actor and a member of the Barrymore family of actors, which included his father and uncles.

John Drew Barrymore Date of birth and death

When was John Drew Barrymore born? When did John Drew Barrymore die? John Drew Barrymore was born on 4th June 1932 and died on 29th November 2004.

John Drew Barrymore children

Who were John Drew Barrymore‘s children? John Drew Barrymore had four children before he died. They were Drew Barrymore, John Blyth Barrymore, Jessica Blyth Barrymore and Blyth Dolores Barrymore.

John Drew Barrymore education

Which school did John Drew Barrymore attend? John Drew Barrymore attended Hollywood Professional School.

John Drew Barrymore movies

What were some of John Drew Barrymore‘s popular movies? John Drew Barrymore made several popular movies before the time of his death. Some are;

  • High Lonesome (1950)
  • Never Love a Stranger (1958)
  • The Big Night (1951)
  • Winchester ‘73 (1967)
  • The Cossacks (1960)
